# CRUX  
### Analytical Training Intelligence for Gym & Sport Climbers

---

## 1. Brand Overview

**Crux** is a premium, serious climbing partner AI designed for gym climbing and outdoor sport climbing.  
It focuses on **analytical coaching**, **training insights**, and **clear beta**, without distraction or hype.

Crux feels like:
- A calm belay partner
- A quiet coach reviewing your session after you untie
- A technical notebook that understands climbing deeply

Crux is not:
- A social network
- A gamified fitness app
- A loud or motivational coach

---

## 2. Brand Personality

### Core Traits
- **Analytical**
- **Encouraging (subtle)**
- **Trustworthy**
- **Precise**
- **Quietly confident**

### Tone of Voice
- Short, declarative sentences
- Neutral, professional language
- No emojis
- No exclamation points
- No unsolicited advice unless enabled

**Examples**
> “Your success rate increases after two practice attempts.”  
> “This move is consistently your crux.”  
> “You rested longer before successful sends.”

---

## 3. Design Pillars

1. **Trust & Safety** – Nothing playful or chaotic  
2. **Clarity Under Load** – Calm visuals during sessions  
3. **Climber-Native Language** – Uses real climbing concepts  
4. **Restraint** – Minimal UI, minimal motion, minimal color  

---

## 4. Color System

### Primary Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Background | Anchor Blue | `#0F1E2E` |
| Primary Text | Chalk White | `#F4F4F2` |
| Secondary Text | Slate Grey | `#8A93A0` |
| Accent / Success | Soft Gold | `#C9A24D` |
| Warning (Rare) | Muted Rust | `#8B4A3B` |

**Rules**
- Dark mode is default
- Soft Gold is used sparingly (<5% of UI)
- No gradients
- No gloss
- Matte surfaces only

---

## 6. Symbol System (No Mascot)

Crux does **not** use a character mascot.

### The Crux Mark
- Two rope-like lines crossing into a subtle **X**
- Ends taper like rope fibers
- Symbolizes:
  - Decision point
  - Tension
  - Precision

**Usage**
- App icon
- Send confirmations
- Insight highlights
- Brand watermark (very subtle)

## 8. Typography

### Primary Typeface
**Inter**
- Excellent readability
- Clean numeric clarity
- Neutral and professional

### Optional Secondary Typeface
**IBM Plex Sans**
- Slightly academic
- Technical tone
- Use for headings only

---

## 9. App Structure (Android)

### Home Screen
- Anchor Blue background
- One dominant action: **Start Session**
- Secondary access:
  - Training
  - Insights
- No feed
- No notifications clutter

---

### Session Mode (Gym / Sport)

- Ultra-minimal UI
- Manual input only (tap / swipe)
- Large typography
- No animations that demand attention

**Session States**
- Attempt started
- Attempt ended
- Rest
- Send

---

## 10. AI Coaching & Training Insights

### Core Focus
- Pattern recognition
- Attempt efficiency
- Movement-specific trends
- Fatigue and rest correlation

### Insight Examples
- “Your highest success rate is on routes under 18 moves.”
- “You fall most often after right-hand gastons.”
- “Three attempts appears to be your optimal limit.”

### Data Visualization Rules
- Line charts only
- Dot plots for distributions
- Time-based views
- No pie charts
- No bar charts

---

## 11. Motion & Interaction

- Slow, weighted transitions
- Ease-in / ease-out only
- No bounce
- No spring
- Motion should feel *heavy and deliberate*

---

## 12. Premium Signals

Crux communicates value through restraint:

- No ads
- No streaks
- No gamification
- No social feed

Instead:
- Long-term trend analysis
- Exportable data
- Precision language
- Clear ownership of your data

---

## 13. App Icon (Android)

- Matte Anchor Blue background
- Centered Crux Mark (rope-line X)
- Chalk White stroke
- No text
- No shadow

---

## 14. What Crux Never Does

- Interrupts mid-climb
- Pushes unsolicited beta
- Uses hype language
- Uses emojis
- Competes for attention

---

## 15. One-Sentence Brand Definition

**Crux is a quiet, analytical climbing partner that helps you understand your climbing — and improve it — without ever getting in the way.**
